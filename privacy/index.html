Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

OnlineFontGenerator.org is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect the minimum possible data to operate our service.

What We Collect

  • Anonymous analytics via Cloudflare: page views, browser type, and country. No personal identifiers.
  • Local Storage: your theme preference and favourite font styles are stored in your browser only — they never leave your device.
  • Text input: text you type is processed entirely in your browser. We never transmit, store, or log it.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set technical cookies for security and performance.

Third Parties

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google's font API may log requests per their privacy policy. We may display ads via Google AdSense.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. You can clear your local storage at any time via your browser settings.
